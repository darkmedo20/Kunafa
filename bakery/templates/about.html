About Us

Based in Doha, Qatar, Kunafa is a private bakery, focused on traditional recipes served with a modern twist.

The chef draw inspiration from Sudanese, Egyptian, and Qatari culture and have a menu of 12-15 items that he rotate seasonally.

The bakery has a rustic and relaxed atmosphere with moderate prices, making it a popular place for a meal any time of the day.

Kunafa is owned by an IT professional, Ahmed Almostafa, who moved to Qatar to pursue his dream of owning a bakery.

To craft the menu, Ahmed Almostafa relies on family recipes and his experience as an entrepreneur in Qatar.

He does all the marketing for the bakery and led the effort to expand the menu beyond classic Qatar to incorporate additional cuisines from the mediterranean region.
